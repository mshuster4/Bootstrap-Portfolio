About Me

Marguerite Shuster is an aspiring web developer with professional experience in multiple industries. Originally from Minneapolis, Minnesota Marguerite graduated in 2013 from University of Minnesota, Twin Cities with a BA in Psychology and Communications. Since leaving academia and moving to San Diego she has worked as a disc jockey, bartender, personal assistant, and dog walker. In her free time, Marguerite enjoys horseback riding, attending concerts and spending time with her dog, Yoshi.
